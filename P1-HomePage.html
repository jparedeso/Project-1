Welcome to Cook-or-Naught...

The app designed for those of us who are very food motivated.

This is your one-stop shop for feeding your face, whether you throw on your apron and get messy, or you leave that to someone else and go out to eat.

Here you can search for recipes for your favorite foods, or just click "I'm Feeling Lucky" at the top and we'll give you a random recipe to try.

Any recipe you try and like or want to save and try another time you're feeling frisky, show it some love and click the little heart to add them to your favorites page. You can do this by signing in to create an account.

Now, we've all gotten elbow deep in flour, or closed the oven door too hard and our soufflé falls, tried to get just the right crust on the crème brûlée with the torch and now the fire department is at our house, or we don't want to adult and would rather say screw it and just go out to eat. Either way we've got you covered. You don't have to waste time switching apps, just click "Go Out" and we'll provide a map for restaurants near you where you can get what you want.

Ok, you now that you've got the gist, channel your inner Julia Child or Martha Stewart and get chopping....or naught!
